1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, use our AI generation tools, or communicate with us. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile settings.
  • Input Content: Any prompts, text, or data you input into our AI generation tools.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments and subscriptions (processed securely via our payment partners).

2. How We Use Data

Your data helps us provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Generate high-quality AI results based on your prompts.
  • Personalize your experience and remember your preferences.
  • Protect against fraudulent or unauthorized activity.
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize our AI models.

3. Generated Content & Public Results

Transparency is key to our community-driven platform.

By default, content generated on MakeAIBucks is set to Public. This allows other users to learn from prompts and build upon successful generations.

"You have full control over your creations. At any time, you can toggle your results to Private in your dashboard settings or permanently Delete any generated content from our servers."

4. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

We respect your privacy rights and provide you with reasonable access to the Personal Data that you may have provided through your use of the Services. Your principal rights under data protection law are:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Erasure

Ask us to delete your personal data from our systems.

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Essential cookies are required for account login and security, while analytical cookies help us understand how you use our platform. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@makeaibucks.com.
